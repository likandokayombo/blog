# My honest opinion on the "Under Different Skies EP" by Elina

When it comes to **"Follow You"**, I think Elina was telling a story of a person who is ready to go anywhere as long as it is called home. This person is willing to leave everything behind and follow.  

As for me, I really connected to the song in the sense that I have reached that point many times  wanting to escape from all the bad things and go somewhere as long as there is a shelter, a place to call home. The reasons being, when life hits rock bottom and takes away the people you love, you might just be willing to take a risk and go where life takes you, even if it means staying under the sun.  

Another track from the EP that stood out to me is **"Toys"**. In this song, Elina explores the complexities of relationships and how attachments, both emotional and material, can shape our experiences. To me, "Toys" felt like a reflection on nostalgia, innocence, and the things we hold on to that no longer serve us. The soft melodies and airy production create a bittersweet feeling  a sense of longing mixed with acceptance. Listening to it, I found myself reflecting on the small, meaningful things I sometimes cling to in my own life and how letting go can be both painful and liberating.  

Overall, the **"Under Different Skies EP"** feels intimate and honest, a glimpse into Elina's world and emotions. Each track carries its own story, but together they paint a vivid picture of vulnerability, growth, and the human experience.
